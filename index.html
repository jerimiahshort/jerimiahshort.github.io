About Me

Welcome! My name is Jerimiah Short and I'm the creative and technical mind behind designs by Jerimiah. I'm glad you stopped by, and I wouldn't want that visit to be wasted, so I wanted to tell you all a little bit about myself.

So I guess I'll start at the beginning. I was born in the small city of Anderson, Indiana to my two loving parents, Tim Short and Brenda (Stewart) Short. We moved around quite a bit growing up, but we eventually landed right back here in Anderson. Since then, my parents were divorced and both have remarried. I love all four of my parents and I wouldn't be who I am today without all of their help and input!

So enough of the personal, what about the love of computers? That all started back when I was around 12 years old. We had just recently moved back to Indiana and I was introduced to my first computer in middle school; an Apple ][e. Most people will more than likely remember these as the wonderful computers that seemed to only play Oregon Trail. While I enjoyed the gaming side of it, I was also intrigued by the “how” of things. My parents noticed my interest and bought me my first computer; a Radio Shack TSR-80 Color Computer II. I started off by copying code from any coding magazine I could get my little paws on. Eventually, I ended up trying to write a little code on my own and the monster was born! Well, I wouldn't call an infinite looping “Hello World” program a monster creator, but it definitely sparked my passion for creating. After many years and several computers, I finally decided this new “internet” thing was really cool. At this point, I was in high school and there was a special club for developing and maintaining the school website was created. I immediately joined and had the time of my life! Looking back, I have fond memories, but also cringe at all the <frameset>s.

Since then, I have been developing websites and graphics for friends and family members. In 2010, I finally decided that making pizzas for the local pizza joint just wasn't what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. It was at this point I coined the name “designs by Jerimiah.” Since then, I have been working hard to showcase my talents and work with local businesses to get them online with professional websites and logos.

So I guess, as they say, the rest is history! Again, welcome to my site and enjoy your stay!
